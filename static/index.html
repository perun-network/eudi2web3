Trusted identity proofs
for Cardano applications

EUDI4Cardano bridges European Digital Identity wallets with the Cardano ecosystem. Submit your Cardano address, authenticate with your EUDI wallet, and generate a privacy-preserving zero-knowledge proof of your credential - ready to publish on-chain.

Step 01

Enter your Cardano address

Submit your address to the server. It will request your EUDI credential and create a zero-knowledge proof that can be published on-chain.

Select the zero-knowledge proof circuit to use for verification.

When checked, your zero-knowledge proof will be published as a transaction on the Cardano blockchain.

Step 02

Authenticate with your EUDI Wallet

Scan the QR code with your EUDI-compliant wallet, or tap the link below to complete authentication and credential verification.

or open directly Open with your wallet
Step 03

Zero-Knowledge Proof Generation

Your credential is being processed on the server. A privacy-preserving zero-knowledge proof is being generated - this may take a moment.
